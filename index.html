Sellers

 

Are you selling a home in the Triangle?

 

Now is a great time to sell a home in the Triangle. We are in a "Sellers' Market", however, when interest rates rise, it may become more difficult to sell a home.

 

For our sellers, to actively market your biggest investment Hannah-Taylor Team will:

  • Have your home professionally measured to ensure that the square footage is accurate. Many real estate agents depend on use square footage from tax records which are often wrong to determine square footage, making the list price less than it should be.
  • Use professional photography to market your property. This is extremely important for getting your property sold.
  • Recommend the most effective improvements to be done to the property to get you the best return on your biggest investment.
  • Use the latest technology and tools available to determine the proper probable sales price that will get your house sold.
  • Advocate for only you. We aren't after the double commission of representing the buyer and seller simultaneously. We are here to work for you and only you.




Buyers

 

Are you buying a home in the Triangle? We are here to represent you.

 

Now is a great time to buy real estate in the Triangle. Although we are in a "Seller's Market", interest rates have never been so low.

 

Don't know where to start with the mortgage process? Hannah-Taylor Team will work with you to find the best lender for you.

 

Hannah-Taylor Team will:

  • Create a customized search with real-time alerts directly from the Triangle MLS catered to your specifications. Now you can stop falling in love with properties that are already under contract or off the market.
  • Negotiate hard to get you the best possible price for the property you select.
  • Advocate for you and only you.
  • Analyze the market using the latest technology and tools.
  • Work to make sure the home sales price is fair based on current market statistics.
  • Coordinate home inspections and appraisals for you to further assure you are getting what you are paying for.
  • Buyers pay nothing for our services. Commissions are paid by the seller. Plus, we don't charge "paperwork" fees like other real estate firms in the Triangle.
